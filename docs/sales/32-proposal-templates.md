# Lean-AI — Proposal Templates

*Two value-framed proposals: the Diagnostic (the front door) and the Automation Sprint (the build). Fill the brackets; keep the structure.*

Both proposals are short — one to two pages. They sell outcomes, not hours. Branded like the website: serif headers, generous white space, one gold accent (see `docs/06-brand-and-positioning.md`). Never put an hourly rate on the page. Send within 24 hours of a qualified fit call, signed by the founder.

Pricing is fixed (EUR, net of VAT): Diagnostic from €4,500; Automation Sprint from €12,000; the Diagnostic fee is fully creditable toward a Sprint.

## Template A — The Diagnostic Proposal

> **Lean-AI — The Diagnostic**
> Prepared for **[Company]** · [Date] · Valid 14 days

### Situation

[Company] runs [the process] roughly [N times/week]. Today it's handled [manually / across X systems / by Y people] and costs an estimated **~€[Y]/year** in loaded time and rework, plus [delay / errors / capacity pressure]. You know AI could help, but you don't yet have a clear, costed answer to *what to build, in what order, and whether it pays back.*

### Desired outcome

A clear, evidence-based decision. By the end you'll know exactly which process to automate first, what the build looks like, the expected payback, and how your data is handled — so the build decision is obvious, not a leap of faith.

### Scope

Over 2–3 weeks we will:

- **Map** [the process] end to end with the people who run it.
- **Simplify** it with Lean — remove waste *before* automating, so we don't automate the mess.
- **Identify and prioritise** the highest-payback automation opportunities.
- **Quantify** the business case: current cost, expected time/error reduction, payback period.
- **Define the data-handling approach** — what data is needed, where it stays, how it's protected.
- **Recommend** a scoped Automation Sprint (or tell you honestly if a build isn't warranted).

### Approach (the Lean-AI Method)

**Map → Simplify → Build → Hand over.** The Diagnostic covers Map and Simplify and scopes the Build. We work *with* your team, not around them — adoption starts here.

### Timeline

| Week | Milestone |
|---|---|
| 1 | Kickoff; interviews; process mapping |
| 2 | Lean analysis; opportunity sizing; data-handling plan |
| 2–3 | Roadmap + business case; readout session |

### Investment

**€4,500** (net of VAT), payable in full on signature.

> **Fully creditable:** if you proceed with the recommended Automation Sprint, this €4,500 is credited in full toward the Sprint. The Diagnostic is a paid scoping step, not a sunk cost.

### What's included

- All interviews, mapping, and Lean analysis
- Prioritised opportunity roadmap
- Quantified business case with expected payback
- Written data-handling approach
- A 60-minute readout and Q&A
- A scoped Sprint recommendation

### What's not included

- Building or deploying any system (that's the Sprint)
- Software licences or third-party tooling fees
- Ongoing support (that's the Operating Partner retainer)

### Terms (summary)

- Fixed price, fixed scope; €4,500 net, due in full on signature.
- Full amount credited toward an Automation Sprint if booked within 90 days.
- We start once payment clears. Mutual NDA available on request.

## Template B — The Automation Sprint Proposal

> **Lean-AI — Automation Sprint**
> Prepared for **[Company]** · [Date] · Valid 14 days

### Situation

The Diagnostic confirmed it: [the process] costs [Company] ~**€[Y]/year** in [time / errors / delay], and the highest-payback fix is to [build the system: e.g. an AI-assisted intake-to-record workflow that ingests X, validates Y, and posts to Z with a human check on exceptions]. The roadmap is clear; this Sprint builds it.

### Desired outcome

A running system your team owns. We target **[specific, measured outcome — e.g. cutting [process] from [3 days] to [under 30 minutes], removing ~[70%] of the manual effort]**, instrumented so you can see it working — handed over with documentation and training.

### Scope

We will build and deploy:

- [Component 1 — e.g. automated intake from email/portal with extraction and validation]
- [Component 2 — e.g. AI-agent step that drafts/classifies/routes with rules]
- [Component 3 — e.g. human-in-the-loop review for exceptions]
- [Component 4 — e.g. write-back to [system of record] and dashboard]
- SOPs and team training so ownership transfers cleanly.

### Approach (the Lean-AI Method)

**Build → Hand over.** Done-with-you, then done-for-you: we build alongside your team, instrument outcomes, and hand you the keys — the system, the documentation, and the know-how. Humans stay in the loop wherever judgment matters; you sign off on what's automated and what isn't.

| Validate | 4–5 | Real-data testing; before/after metrics captured |
| Hand over | 5–6 | Training, SOPs, go-live; sign-off on outcomes |
