## Part 2 — Pitch / overview deck outline (11 slides)

Restrained design: one idea per slide, serif headlines, one gold accent, lots of white space. Headline carries the message; body is sparse.

### Slide 1 — Title
**Headline:** Lean-AI — *The operating partner for the AI era.*
**Content:** Wordmark on Ink background, gold accent. Sub-line: "We build the AI-native operating systems behind great companies." Founder name + hello@lean-ai.com.

### Slide 2 — The problem
**Headline:** Most SMEs aren't behind on AI. They're stuck.
**Content:** They know it matters; they lack the team to execute. The result: hype, pilots that die, repetitive work still eating the team's week. Name the prospect's reality.

### Slide 3 — Why the usual options fail
**Headline:** Consultants advise and leave. Tools automate the mess.
**Content:** Three columns — *Consultancies* (a deck, no system), *AI tools / freelancers* (automate broken processes, no ownership), *Doing it internally* (no Lean step, no adoption owner — it stalls). Sets up the gap.

### Slide 4 — What we do
**Headline:** We don't sell advice. We build the system, prove it works, and hand you the keys.
**Content:** Lean-AI builds running AI-native operating systems. Outcomes, not hours. The category: an operations studio / transformation partner — not consulting, not an agency.

### Slide 5 — The method
**Headline:** Map → Simplify → Build → Hand over.
**Content:** Four-step arc, left to right. Map the process · Simplify with Lean · Build with AI agents · Hand over a system your team owns. One line each.

### Slide 6 — Why it's different (proof pillars)
**Headline:** Four things we hold to.
**Content:** Built, not advised · Lean before automation · Adoption is the product · Measured outcomes. The reasons the work actually sticks.

### Slide 7 — Proof / case study
**Headline:** From 3 days to 20 minutes. *(use a real result once available)*
**Content:** One before/after teardown — the metric table from `docs/sales/36-case-study-template.md`. While pre-launch, use the clearly-labelled illustrative example or the "be your own case study" angle: running a premium business in ~12 hrs/week on its own AI systems.

### Slide 8 — Data & trust
**Headline:** AI without handing over your data.
**Content:** Data-handling designed up front — minimisation, residency, sensitive data kept in your environment, human-in-the-loop where judgment matters. Disarms the #1 objection.

### Slide 9 — How to engage
**Headline:** Three steps. The Diagnostic is the front door.
**Content:** The Diagnostic (from €4,500, credited) → Automation Sprint (from €12,000) → Operating Partner (from €3,500/mo). Fixed scope, fixed price. Net of VAT.

### Slide 10 — Who it's for
**Headline:** Built for ambitious SMEs with a process worth fixing.
**Content:** 10–250 people, DACH/EU, a painful repetitive high-volume process, a decision-maker who values ownership. Honest line: if it's not a fit, we'll tell you.

### Slide 11 — Call to action
**Headline:** Start with a fit call.
**Content:** 30 minutes, no pitch, an honest read on whether it's worth fixing. **hello@lean-ai.com · lean-ai.com.** Wordmark + gold accent to close.
