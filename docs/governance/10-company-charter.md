# Lean-AI — Company Charter

*Why Lean-AI exists, how it decides, and the contract the founder keeps with themselves.*

---

## 1. Mission

Turn AI from hype into operations that actually run — for ambitious small and mid-sized companies in DACH and the EU that know AI matters but lack the team to execute it.

> *We don't sell advice. We build the system, prove it works, and hand you the keys.*

## 2. Vision

To be the **operating partner for the AI era**: the studio SMEs call when they want a measured, AI-native operating system they own — not a deck, not a dependency. A premium, senior-only practice that can stay a high-margin side business or scale to a small senior team, with options open and no debt.

---

## 3. Core values

1. **Built, not advised.** We ship running systems. A deliverable that doesn't run isn't done. *(Operational excellence.)*
2. **Lean before automation.** We remove the waste first; we never automate a broken process. Simplicity is the work. *(Lean.)*
3. **Adoption is the product.** The client's team must own the system after we leave. We lead by serving them, transferring skill and authority — not creating reliance. *(Servant leadership.)*
4. **Measured outcomes.** We instrument what we build and orient to payback. Claims are backed by numbers. *(Operational excellence.)*
5. **Honesty over the upsell.** We say what we'd do if it were our money, decline bad-fit work, and surface bad news early. The Diagnostic is a paid sales process credited back — not a profit trap. *(Honesty.)*

---

## 4. Operating principles

- **Fixed scope, fixed price.** Three shapes only: Diagnostic, Sprint, Operating Partner. No bespoke negotiation.
- **WIP-limited.** One active build at a time + up to ~2 retainers. A queue is a premium signal, not a problem.
- **Async-first.** Written briefs and recorded walkthroughs over live meetings.
- **Reuse compounds.** Every client build becomes a parameterised template; marginal delivery cost falls while price holds.
- **Eat our own dog food.** Lean-AI runs on the systems it sells; our internal numbers are the headline case study.
- **Premium, never cheap.** We raise prices before we add volume. We charge value, never hours.

---

## 5. What Lean-AI does — and does NOT do

**Does**
- Map, simplify, and build AI-native operating systems for SME operations.
- Deliver fixed-scope Diagnostics, Sprints, and Operating Partner retainers.
- Transfer ownership: documentation, training, handover.

**Does NOT**
- Sell strategy decks or advice without a built system.
- Take hourly or open-ended "staff augmentation" work.
- Automate broken processes, or "move fast and break" a client's operations.
- Build consumer products, do generic web/agency work, or chase logos that don't fit.
- Compete on price, raise outside money, or take on debt.
- Exceed the WIP limit to "squeeze one in."
- Work without a cleared deposit, or before written employer permission for client #1.

---

## 6. Decision-making principles (solo founder)

- **Default to the WIP limit and the time budget.** If a decision threatens either, the answer is no — or "next start date is X."
- **One-way vs. two-way doors.** Move fast on reversible decisions; sleep on irreversible ones (legal form, hiring, big pricing changes).
- **Time-protective by default.** When two options are close, pick the one that costs fewer founder hours.
- **Premium tie-breaker.** When unsure, choose the option that strengthens the premium positioning, not the one that maximises this month's revenue.
- **Outsource judgement where required.** Tax/legal/VAT decisions go to a Steuerberater; the charter is not a substitute for advice.
- **Decline gracefully.** A clear, kind "not a fit" protects the brand and the calendar.

---

## 7. The operating contract with myself

A personal commitment that keeps the day job, quality, and health intact.

**Time discipline**
- Cap Lean-AI at **10–15 hrs/week**. If hours creep past 15 for two weeks running, **raise prices or pause intake** — never just absorb it.
- Protect deep-build blocks; keep client calls to a minimum.

**WIP limit**
- **One active build + up to ~2 retainers.** Everything else queues. No exceptions for "easy" or "urgent" work.

**Pricing rules**
- Never quote below list: Diagnostic from €4,500, Sprint from €12,000, Operating Partner from €3,500/mo (net of VAT).
- Never bill by the hour on an invoice.
- Diagnostic fee fully creditable to a Sprint.
- Raise prices ~10–15% once at ≥80% capacity, before adding volume.

**Integrity gates**
- Written employer permission before invoicing client #1.
- No conflict of interest with the employer (clients, sectors, time).
- Deposit clears before delivery starts.
- Bad news to clients early and plainly.

> If a decision violates this contract, it is the wrong decision — regardless of the revenue attached to it.
