# Lean-AI — Executive Summary

*The operating partner for the AI era.*

---

**What it is.** Lean-AI is a premium, AI-native **operations studio** for ambitious small and mid-sized companies. We design and *build* working operating systems — combining **AI agents**, **Lean methodology**, **servant leadership**, and **operational excellence** into one repeatable method. We don't sell decks; every engagement ends with a running system the client owns.

**Why now.** The market is large and fast-growing (AI-agent services ~45% CAGR), but the German *Mittelstand* and EU SMEs are under-served: ~28% mid-sized / ~17% small-firm AI adoption, and ~43% of mid-sized firms have *no concrete AI plan*. They have intent but lack skills, trust, and clarity. Lean-AI converts that intent into outcomes.

**Who's behind it.** Founder **Stefan Gruber** — a Global Head of Engineering with 20+ years in regulated pharmaceutical manufacturing, a **Lean Six Sigma Black Belt** with an Executive MBA (IESE), who has governed $400M+ CAPEX portfolios and led 850+-person engineering organizations. The same operational-excellence playbook (OTIF +32%, reliability +11%, ~$30M annual savings), now packaged for SMEs. See `governance/13-founder-profile.md`.

**How it makes money.** Three productized, fixed-price offers:
- **The Diagnostic** — €4,500, 2–3 wks (paid front door, creditable to a Sprint).
- **Automation Sprint** — €12,000, 4–6 wks (the build).
- **Operating Partner** — €3,500/mo (the recurring, compounding engine).

**Why it fits a part-time founder (10–15 hrs/wk).** Productized scope + a hard work-in-progress limit (one active build + ~2 retainers) + async-first delivery + heavy internal AI leverage. First hire is a flat-rate subcontractor, only once retainers fund it.
