## 1. Purpose and positioning

1.1 Lean-AI is an operations studio. We build running systems; we do not merely advise. Our method follows a four-move arc — **Map → Simplify → Build → Hand over** — drawing on AI agents, Lean, servant leadership, and operational excellence.

1.2 This Agreement sets out the general terms that govern all services Lean-AI provides to the Client. It does not, by itself, oblige either Party to order or perform any specific work. Specific work is commissioned exclusively through individual Statements of Work (see Section 2).

---

## 2. Structure: framework plus Statements of Work

2.1 **Framework.** This Agreement is a framework agreement (*Rahmenvertrag*). The concrete scope, deliverables, timeline, fees, and acceptance criteria of any engagement are defined in a separate **Statement of Work** (**"SOW"** / *Leistungsschein*) executed by both Parties.

2.2 **Incorporation.** Each signed SOW incorporates and is governed by this Agreement. Together, this Agreement and a given SOW form the entire contract for that engagement.

2.3 **Order of precedence.** If a conflict arises between this Agreement and a SOW, this Agreement prevails, **except** where the SOW expressly states that a specific clause of this Agreement is amended for that engagement and identifies the clause by number.

2.4 **No exclusivity.** Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party exclusivity. Lean-AI may serve other clients, including within the Client's industry, subject to Section 8 (Confidentiality).

## 3. Scope of services

3.1 Lean-AI will perform the services described in each SOW (the **"Services"**), which may include the following offers (prices net of VAT):

- **The Diagnostic** — from €4,500, typically 2–3 weeks. We map your operations, find the constraint, and deliver a prioritised build plan.
- **Automation Sprint** — from €12,000, typically 4–6 weeks. We build, test, and hand over a working automated system for a defined process.
- **Operating Partner** — from €3,500 per month, minimum commitment of one quarter. Ongoing operation, measurement, and iteration of your systems.

3.2 **Performance standard.** Lean-AI will perform the Services with the care and skill reasonably expected of a competent provider of comparable services. Unless a SOW expressly states otherwise, the Services are rendered as a contract for services (*Dienstvertrag*) and not as a contract for a guaranteed work product (*Werkvertrag*); where a SOW defines acceptance criteria for a defined deliverable, that deliverable is treated as a work result (*Werk*) for acceptance purposes only.

3.3 **Cooperation-dependent.** Delivery depends on the Client's timely cooperation as set out in the relevant SOW and in Section 9. Delays attributable to the Client extend Lean-AI's deadlines accordingly and may affect fees as set out in Section 4.

## 4. Fees, deposits and payment

4.1 **Fees.** The Client will pay the fees stated in each SOW. All fees are stated **net of value-added tax (VAT / USt.)**. Statutory VAT, where applicable, is added and shown separately on each invoice.

4.2 **Deposits and billing schedule.** Unless a SOW states otherwise:

- **The Diagnostic** — 100% of the fee is invoiced upfront upon signature of the SOW; work begins on receipt of payment.
- **Automation Sprint** — 50% of the fee is invoiced upfront upon signature of the SOW; the remaining 50% is invoiced on acceptance of the deliverables under Section 6.
- **Operating Partner** — the monthly retainer is invoiced monthly **in advance**, with a minimum commitment of one calendar quarter.

4.3 **Payment terms.** All invoices are due **net 14 days** from the invoice date, payable by bank transfer to the account stated on the invoice, without deduction.

4.4 **Late payment.** If the Client fails to pay a due amount, the Client is in default in accordance with §§ 286, 288 BGB. Lean-AI may charge statutory default interest and, in the case of B2B transactions, a flat default fee in accordance with § 288 (5) BGB. Lean-AI may suspend the Services after `[NUMBER]` days' written notice of overdue payment until the account is brought current.

4.5 **No set-off.** The Client may set off only with claims that are undisputed or have been finally adjudicated, and may exercise a right of retention only based on claims arising from the same contractual relationship.

## 12. Term and termination

12.1 **Term of the framework.** This Agreement starts on the Effective Date and continues until terminated. Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on `[30]` days' written notice, provided no SOW is then in effect.

12.2 **Project SOWs (Diagnostic, Sprint).** A fixed-scope SOW runs until the Deliverables are accepted or the engagement is otherwise completed. Either Party may terminate a project SOW for convenience on `[14]` days' written notice; in that case the Client pays for Services properly performed up to the effective date of termination, and upfront amounts already invoiced for work performed are non-refundable.

12.3 **Operating Partner (retainer).** The retainer has a **minimum commitment of one calendar quarter**. After the minimum term, either Party may terminate at the end of any calendar month on `[30]` days' written notice (i.e. ordinarily to the end of the following month).

12.4 **Termination for cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for good cause (*außerordentliche Kündigung aus wichtigem Grund*, § 314 BGB), including a material breach not cured within `[14]` days of written notice, or the other Party's insolvency.

12.5 **Effect of termination.** On termination, the Client pays all fees due for Services performed and approved expenses incurred up to the effective date. Sections 7, 8, 9, 11, and 14 survive termination. Upon full payment, Lean-AI will hand over the then-current Deliverables and reasonable handover materials.
