## 1. Scope by Work Order
1.1 Lean-AI may, from time to time, offer the Subcontractor defined assignments by issuing a written **Work Order** describing the deliverables, the agreed result, the flat-rate fee or milestones, and the delivery date. No assignment exists until a Work Order is accepted by both Parties in text form.
1.2 This Agreement is a framework; it does not oblige Lean-AI to offer, or the Subcontractor to accept, any assignment. The Subcontractor is free to decline any Work Order.
1.3 Each accepted Work Order incorporates this Agreement. In a conflict, this Agreement prevails unless the Work Order expressly amends a numbered clause.

## 2. Manner of performance
2.1 The Subcontractor determines its own working time, place, and methods, and is **not** subject to instructions beyond the definition of the desired result and reasonable coordination of interfaces, deadlines, and client-facing quality requirements.
2.2 The Subcontractor is not integrated into Lean-AI's organisation, uses its own equipment and tools, and may engage its own staff or sub-subcontractors (subject to Sections 6 and 7 and Lean-AI's prior written consent for client-data access).
2.3 The Subcontractor may work for other principals and is not granted, and does not expect, exclusivity.

## 3. Fees — flat-rate / milestone basis
3.1 Compensation is **flat-rate per Work Order or per defined milestone — not hourly and not time-based.** Each Work Order states the fixed fee for the agreed result.
3.2 Fees are **net of VAT**; statutory VAT, where applicable, is added and shown separately. The Subcontractor is solely responsible for correctly invoicing VAT and for its own taxes and social-security contributions.
3.3 The Subcontractor invoices on completion and acceptance of each Work Order or milestone. Invoices are payable **net `[14]` days**. Fees cover all of the Subcontractor's costs unless a Work Order expressly pre-approves specific third-party expenses, which are then reimbursed at cost against receipts.

## 4. Independent contractor; caution on Scheinselbstständigkeit
4.1 The Parties intend an independent-contractor relationship (*freie/selbstständige Tätigkeit*). This Agreement does **not** establish an employment relationship, partnership, or agency. The Subcontractor receives no holiday pay, sick pay, continued remuneration, or other employee benefits.
4.2 **Caution (Scheinselbstständigkeit).** Both Parties acknowledge that the legal classification of self-employment depends on how the relationship is actually lived, not only on this wording. To support genuine self-employment, the Parties will, in practice: keep work organised by result via Work Orders rather than by working hours; avoid integrating the Subcontractor into Lean-AI's fixed work organisation; allow the Subcontractor to work for other clients; and avoid making Lean-AI the Subcontractor's sole or near-sole source of income on a sustained basis. The Subcontractor is encouraged to maintain multiple clients. The Parties may use the status-determination procedure (*Statusfeststellungsverfahren*) of the Deutsche Rentenversicherung where appropriate. **Misclassification can trigger back-payment of social-security contributions and tax liabilities; each Party should take its own professional advice.**
4.3 The Subcontractor confirms it is properly registered for its trade/profession and meets its own statutory obligations (taxes, insurance, social security, and any required professional liability insurance of at least `[AMOUNT]`).

## 5. Intellectual property — full assignment to Lean-AI
5.1 To the maximum extent permissible under German law, the Subcontractor assigns to Lean-AI all transferable rights, title, and interest in all work results, deliverables, code, configurations, prompts, designs, and materials created under any Work Order (the **"Work Results"**), and grants Lean-AI the exclusive, transferable, sublicensable, unlimited (in time, territory, and content) right of use (*ausschließliches, übertragbares, unbeschränktes Nutzungsrecht*, §§ 31, 31a UrhG) in all known and unknown types of use, including the right to modify and to onward-assign to Lean-AI's clients.
5.2 The assignment and rights grant are fully covered by the agreed fee and take effect on creation; payment of the corresponding Work Order fee confirms them. The Subcontractor waives any right to be named as author to the extent legally waivable.
5.3 The Subcontractor will not retain or reuse Work Results or any client-specific materials for other clients. Pre-existing tools of the Subcontractor that are embedded in a Work Result must be disclosed in advance; if used, the Subcontractor grants Lean-AI a perpetual, royalty-free, sublicensable licence to use them as part of the Work Result.
5.4 The Subcontractor warrants the Work Results are original or properly licensed and do not infringe third-party rights, and will procure all necessary third-party and open-source clearances.

## 6. Confidentiality
6.1 The Subcontractor will keep strictly confidential all non-public information of Lean-AI and of Lean-AI's clients (including business, technical, financial, and personal data, methods, prompts, and the existence and content of engagements), use it only to perform the Work Orders, and protect it with at least reasonable care.
6.2 The exclusions, compelled-disclosure handling, and return/destruction obligations mirror standard practice: obligations do not apply to information that is public without breach, lawfully known beforehand, independently developed, or rightfully received from a third party; legally compelled disclosure is permitted with prior notice where lawful; and on request or termination the Subcontractor returns or securely destroys all such information and copies.
6.3 Confidentiality survives termination for `[3]` years; trade secrets remain protected under the GeschGehG for as long as they qualify. A separate NDA, if signed, supplements this Section.

## 7. Client non-solicitation and non-circumvention
7.1 During the term and for `[12]` months thereafter, the Subcontractor will not, directly or indirectly, solicit, accept, or perform work for any Lean-AI client (or its affiliates) that the Subcontractor became aware of, or worked with, through Lean-AI, nor otherwise circumvent Lean-AI to contract directly with such clients, without Lean-AI's prior written consent.
7.2 The Subcontractor will not solicit Lean-AI's other subcontractors or personnel for `[12]` months after the term.
7.3 For each breach of 7.1 or 7.2, the Subcontractor owes a contractual penalty of `[AMOUNT]`, without prejudice to Lean-AI's right to claim higher proven damages and injunctive relief.

## 8. Data protection
8.1 The Subcontractor will comply with the GDPR (DSGVO) and the BDSG. Where the Subcontractor processes personal data on behalf of Lean-AI or a Lean-AI client, the Subcontractor will conclude a Data Processing Agreement (*Auftragsverarbeitungsvertrag*, Art. 28 GDPR) before processing begins, and will implement appropriate technical and organisational measures and engage further processors only with prior written authorisation.
8.2 The Subcontractor will not transfer personal data to third countries without a valid transfer mechanism and Lean-AI's prior written approval.

## 9. Quality standards
9.1 Work Results must meet the professional standard expected of a competent specialist and conform to the agreed result, the relevant client's acceptance criteria, and Lean-AI's reasonable quality and documentation requirements communicated in advance.
9.2 Lean-AI (or its client) will review Work Results against the Work Order; the Subcontractor will remedy material defects at no extra charge within a reasonable period. Acceptance follows the criteria stated in the Work Order. Statutory rights for defects (*Mängelrechte*) remain unaffected.

## 10. Liability and insurance
10.1 The Subcontractor is liable for damages it causes in accordance with statutory provisions, and indemnifies Lean-AI against third-party claims (including client claims) arising from the Subcontractor's breach, defective Work Results, IP infringement, or breach of confidentiality or data protection, save where caused by Lean-AI. The Subcontractor maintains adequate professional liability insurance.

## 11. Term and termination
11.1 This Agreement runs from the Effective Date for an indefinite term and may be terminated by either Party on `[30]` days' written notice; termination does not affect Work Orders already in progress, which run to completion unless separately terminated.
11.2 A Work Order may be terminated for convenience by Lean-AI on `[14]` days' notice, in which case the Subcontractor is paid pro rata for the result actually delivered and accepted up to that point.
11.3 Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any Work Order for good cause (*aus wichtigem Grund*, § 314 BGB), including uncured material breach within `[14]` days of notice, or insolvency.
11.4 Sections 5, 6, 7, 8, and 10 survive termination. On termination, the Subcontractor returns all Lean-AI and client property, materials, access credentials, and data.

## 12. General provisions
12.1 **Governing law / jurisdiction.** German law applies, excluding conflict-of-laws rules and the CISG. Exclusive jurisdiction is `[CITY]` (Lean-AI's seat), to the extent the Subcontractor is a merchant, legal entity under public law, or special fund under public law.
12.2 **Form.** Amendments require text form (*Textform*, § 126b BGB), including any change to this requirement.
12.3 **Severability.** If a provision is invalid, the remainder stays effective and the invalid provision is replaced by one closest to its economic intent.
12.4 **Entire agreement.** This Agreement with its Work Orders (and any NDA/AVV) is the entire agreement on its subject matter. Notices in text form to the addresses above, or to Lean-AI at **hello@lean-ai.com**.
